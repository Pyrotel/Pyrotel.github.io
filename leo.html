Description

Departing from: Chromosphere

A heavenly cruise in the Sun with a 1 bedroom cabin... go on a 3 night trip that is absolutely worthwile. Learn about how the Sun looks on the inside by diving deep into the Sun's Core. Stop at locations including the Photosphere, Convective Zone, and Radiative Zone along the way!

The view is astounding, and it is highly suggested to go on a cruise into the Sun at least once in your life. This trip is reccomended for its easily accessed scientific facts and sites, as well as it's relaxing atmosphere.
