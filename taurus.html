Description

Departing from: Ishtar Terra

A once in a lifetime experience where you set sail around Venus for 8 nights. Stay in a 1 bedroom cabin and visit sites including Sedna Planitia, Alpha Regio, Aino Planitia, Thetis Regio, and Aphrodite Terra. This lovely view has sights that will most certainly catch your eye, so be sure you've brought your camera!

The overwhelmingly dazzling atmosphere of Venus is perfect for those looking for some time to themselves. This cruise is a luxury vacation intended for any one person or smaller group of people looking for a grand time. Lay back and enjoy some time to yourselves on this cruise that is fit for royalty.
